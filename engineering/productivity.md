# Productivity Habits
- **No multitasking**
  - 
- **Take meeting notes**: Take notes during every meeting and send them out to participants for confirmation
  - _Attendees_ and _Excusees_
  - _Date_
  - _Goal_: What answer do you want to get at the end of the meeting
  - _Agenda_: What topics to discuss
  - _Discussion_: Live meeting notes
  - _Conclusion_: Summary of discussion
  - _Action Points_: What actions will be taken and by who
- **Keep a clean desk**: 6 Items and nothing else
  - Laptop
  - Mouse
  - Keyboard
  - Headset
  - Notepad
  - Pen
- **Plan your day ahead**:
  - EoD = Planning next day
  - Time blocking
  - Clear goal per time block: **S**pecific, **M**easureable, **A**chievable, **R**elevant, **T**imely 
- **Keep a to do list**: Keep a to do list and convert your paper notes to your to do list
