
🎯 **Goal**: Reduce the risk of dementia and cognitive decline

**What**
The MIND diet is a combination of the Mediterranean diet and the DASH (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) diet.

**MIND** stands for _**M**editerranean-DASH **I**ntervention for **N**eurodegenerative **D**elay_

**Principles**
- 🍖 No processed meats
- 🧀 No cheese or butter
- 🍪 No added sugars or sweeteners
- 🍟 No fried foods
- 🍷 No alcohol
